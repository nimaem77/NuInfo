About us

Nu Info Systems, Inc. (NuIS) is a complete software solutions provider. We provide a multi-faceted portfolio of products and services to our clients, including a complete E-Commerce solution methodology, web and internet applications, client/server solutions, data warehousing and database management systems, on-site and off-site software systems development, turnkey projects, training, and multimedia development.

Since 1996, we have been the preferred source of information technology for Fortune 1000 firms throughout the world. During this period, we have grown dramatically, from a handful of employees to a peak staffing level of almost a hundred employees and consultants. Our growth has been directed at both broadening our areas of expertise as well as extended our geographic reach, always aiming to better service the needs of our clients.

NuIS's diverse expertise allows us to handle large-scale, multi-dimensional projects. We've received accolades for successfully developing products, from concept to implementation, in record time. Many projects have included project management, development, testing, documentation, technical support, packaging and training. We have successfully adapted to the rapid pace of technological change of the last two decades, and look forward to delivering even more value to our clients as we move together into an exciting new era of software innovation.
